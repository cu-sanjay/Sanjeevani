About Sanjeevani

A student built health literacy project.

Sanjeevani takes its name from the mythical herb said to restore life in Indian legend. The idea behind the project is simple: most people search for their symptoms online long before they see a doctor. Sanjeevani tries to make that first step more useful, more organised and a little more trustworthy than a random search.

The symptom checker, medicine reference, nutrition guide and reminder tools are built on public health datasets. The health assistant is powered by a language model through the Featherless AI API. None of these tools are a substitute for a licensed medical professional.

Built by Sanjay. Source and updates on GitHub.
